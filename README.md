### Features
- **Dual Perspectives**: Choose to play as **The Denizen** (survival/stealth) or **The Occupier** (control/resource management).
- **Resource Management**: Balance Hunger, Suspicion, Control, and Unrest depending on your role.
- **Narrative Events**: Make difficult choices that impact your survival and the city's fate.
